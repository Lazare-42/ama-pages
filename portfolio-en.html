The Situation

Creators, musicians, architects — everyone works in isolation.

Imagine: you want to organize a concert in an unusual venue. You spend three weeks looking for a sound engineer, a videographer, equipment. Every contact is a fresh start. The project happens — but the network dissolves the very next day. Same story everywhere, from Paris to Berlin, Barcelona to London.

Everywhere in the world, thousands of small creative, associative and technical structures share the same needs — venues, equipment, network, visibility — but have no way to coordinate.

SMEs
No cross-sector network
NGOs / Associations
No shared logistics infrastructure
Startups
No pooled resources
Artists
No visibility or shared tools

What we observe

  • Every event starts from scratch: contacts, logistics, comms
  • Tools don't communicate with each other
  • Collaborations stay one-off and get lost
  • No collective memory between projects

What's needed

  • ✓ A common foundation across disciplines
  • ✓ Shared and interconnected tools
  • ✓ A network that accumulates instead of resetting
  • ✓ A lasting infrastructure, not a temporary one

No common infrastructure

Projects last 2-4 years max. When funding ends, the network dissolves

Access barriers

Complex processes, required consortiums, administrative jargon

The AMA Vision

Connecting worlds through action.

Yann, Youhna — different names for the same intention: building bridges. Between disciplines, between cultures, between those who have ideas and those who have the means to make them real.

AMA is not just another project — it's a backbone, a permanent infrastructure on which projects plug in, grow, and connect with each other.

HUBJAM

Music & Sound Engineering

HUBJAM is AMA's music and sound engineering pillar, co-founded with Loïc (sound/light engineer, Salle du Cairn, Larmor-Baden). Modular solutions for performances and sound recordings.

5 device versions (V1–V5), live-tested in Brittany and Paris.

HUBJAM Device — 5 Versions

V1: 25×17 cm — 6 jacks + 4 XLR in / 2 mini-jacks + 2 jacks + 2 XLR out

V3: 2 boxes (stage + control) with sound card and presets

V5: integrated 5G/6G relay (SIM card + router)
