The AMA Project

AMA connects art, music and architecture around ecological transition. Concretely: eco-designed modular spaces (PIN-LAB), low-tech audio gear built to last (HUBJAM), audiovisual documentation (FLOAT). Each participant declares their skills (level 1–3) and receives adapted roles. The goal: prove that creativity and sobriety can coexist, by making the most of existing resources.

Long-Term Vision

METR H2O — Floating Logistics Island Network

In the long term, AMA's modular and logistics expertise feeds into METR H2O: a network of autonomous floating logistics islands. 500m hexagonal structure, 5 functional zones (Z1-Z5), Water Web inter-island connectivity system.
