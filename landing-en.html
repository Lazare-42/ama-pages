What is AMA?

What is it?

AMA is a hybrid ecosystem connecting art, music and architecture around ecological transition. We build modular tools to go from idea to realization.

How?

1. Let's discuss your project.
2. We adapt our tools to your needs.
3. We build together.

Where?

Wherever our tools are used: online, in our workshops (Paris, Brittany), and with our partners.

INDEX — The heart of AMA

INDEX is our central coordination tool. It feeds, pools and connects all our projects.
With INDEX, a PIN-LAB artist can collaborate with a HUBJAM technician for an event managed by FLOAT.

Our Projects
